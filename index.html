About the Program

More than a competition

Young IT Talent is designed to find students who are ready to learn, grow, and step into the tech world with the right support. The competition format helps identify students with real potential.

Why this program exists

Many students have talent but do not know where to start. This program helps discover motivated school students and gives them a real chance to begin their IT journey in a structured way.

How selection works

We use the competition as a smart way to evaluate thinking, potential, motivation, and readiness to learn. The goal is to choose students who are serious about growth and technology.
